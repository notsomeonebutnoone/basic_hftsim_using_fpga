for FPGA active high or active low does not matter as much 
but the time the logic is loaded, the supply voltage is aldready stable. 

for asic, it is different, they should go with what the cell library uses. 
using the other one can incur penalties. (if the cell library is using active high, it should use active high, and vice versa)

on PCB level you almost always want to use active low as it is much easier to keeep the reset nailed to the ground
